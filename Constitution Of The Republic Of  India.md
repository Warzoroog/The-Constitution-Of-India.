Constitution Of The Republic Of India (War Era)

Abbrevations And Definations
For the Purpose of this Constituion:

Acting President — A person temporarily fulfilling the duties of the President after removal, resignation, or inability of the incumbent to serve.
Advisor — Advisor to Government of India.
Amendment — A formal change or addition to the Constitution, subject to the amendment procedures defined in this Constitution.
Attorney General — Head of the Judicial Branch and chief legal officer responsible for constitutional interpretation and legal oversight.
Bill — A proposed law introduced in the Legislative Body for consideration and passage.
Congress — The Legislative Body (Members of Parliament elected by citizens) responsible for making laws, authorizing impeachment,treaties, and other constitutional functions.
Court of Law — Judicial functions established under this Constitution to adjudicate constitutional disputes and violations.
Discord Server — The Official Indian Discord Server where constitutional decision processes (debates and votes) are conducted.
Emergency Powers — Extraordinary authorities exercised by government during national crises, as defined in the Constitution.
Evidence — Screenshots from in‑game or the Official Indian Discord Server provided in case of dispute to support constitutional or legal claims.
Executive Order — A directive issued by the President to manage operations of the government and implement policy within constitutional and legal limits.
Ethics Standards — Rules governing expected conduct of officials to prevent abuse of office and corruption.
Impeachment — The constitutional process by which the President may be charged and removed from office for betrayal, incompetence, or other constitutionally defined grounds.
Inactivity — Failure by an official to perform constitutional duties for a specified period (e.g., 7 days) without valid notice.
Incompetence — A pattern of conduct by an official that fails to fulfill their duties in a manner that protects national interests.
Law/Laws — Includes terms and conditions of the War Era game and Discord server rules.
Majority — More than half of the votes cast by a deliberative body, such as Congress, required for passage of most laws or motions.
Ministers — In‑game and Discord Ministers and Directors responsible for executive functions.
National Security — Security of the Republic of India, including defense and strategic interests.
Other Official Position — Discord Directors and other appointed positions referenced in Article 4.
President — Current President of the Republic of India.
Ratification — Formal confirmation of decisions, treaties, or actions required by constitutional procedures.
Supermajority — A voting threshold greater than a simple majority (e.g.,more than 75% in Favour) required for key constitutional decisions.
Treaties — Binding agreements between India and other nations or entities, subject to legislative approval.
Vice‑President — Vice President of the Republic of India, assisting the President and succeeding when needed.
War — Official military conflict requiring presidential and legislative authorization.


Preamble
 
We,

the Citizens of the Republic of India,

in order to establish justice,

ensure peace,

promote prosperity,

and secure the freedom and rights of all our citizens within War Era,

hereby adopt this Constitution.


Article 1 — Sovereignty and Purpose.

Sovereignty:
The Republic Of India is a Sovereign state,
with authority derived from its citizens,
within the War Era world.

Purpose:
To protect the welfare of its citizens,
promote strategic strength, and
govern the state equitably.


Article 2 — Citizenship, Rights And Duties.
Citizenship:
Every active player who pledges allegiance to the Republic of India is a citizen with rights and responsibilities.

Rights:
Citizens have the right to:
1.Participate in fair elections in-game and on the Discord server.
2.Freedom of speech within in-game and Discord laws with reasonable restrictions.
3.Engage in economic activity such as trade, form companies, and work for companies of their choice.

Duties:
Citizens shall:
1.Abide by The Laws of this Nation.
2.Support the nation’s defense if called upon.
3.Respect the constitution and governance structures of the Republic of India, Whether in game or in Discord Server.


Article 3 — Government Structure
Executive Branch
Includes:
President,
Vice President,
Advisor To Government Of India.

Ministery:
Ministery Of Defense,
Ministery Of Finance,
Ministery Of External Affairs,
Ministery Of Home Affairs and
Other Official Positiions.

Legislative Branch
Shall Include:
Consists of Members of Parliament elected by citizens.
Responsible for passing laws and legislative functions.

Judicial Branch:
Headed by the Attorney General and
assisted by appointed members charged with resolving disputes and 
adjudicating violations of the Constitution and laws.


Article 4 — Elections, Appointment of minsters.
Election Process:
Elections held at designated intervals.
Candidate registration and fair voting processes is defined as per in game rules and Discord rules.

Term Limits:
Officials and other official positions serve a fixed term of 30 days.
Are subject to removal for inactivity, incompetence, or constitutional violations.


Article 5 — Laws and Policy
Lawmaking Procedures:
Bills and Laws may be proposed by Parliament,Ministers Or Other Official Postions.
Passage requires majority vote in Legislative Body.

External Relations:

Article 11 — Enforcement
Compliance:
All citizens and government officials must comply with this Constitution.
Violations are addressed through judicial processes established after adoption of this Constitution.


Article 12 — Impeachment, Resignation, and Emergency Removal

12.1-Grounds for Removal:
The President may be removed for betrayal of national interests, incompetence, or resignation.

12.2 Impeachment:
Only Congress may initiate impeachment.
Motions may be proposed by any Congress member, Minister, or Other Official Position.
Impeachment is first proposed on Discord and debated by Congress.
A simple majority of Congress votes on Discord is required to impeach.
After successful Discord impeachment, the action must be confirmed in‑game before removal is finalized.

12.3 Outcome:
If impeached and confirmed, the President is removed.
The Vice‑President becomes Acting President.
Congress may call a new election if needed.

12.4 Resignation:
Written resignation is submitted; Vice‑President becomes Acting President.
If Vice‑President cannot serve, Congress elects an Acting President by simple majority.

12.5 Emergency Removal:
Congress may remove the President by three‑fourths majority on Discord if actions pose immediate harm.
Vice‑President becomes Acting President and impeachment may follow.

Article 13 — Powers of Congress
Congress may:
Propose, debate, and pass laws
Vote on impeachment and acting appointments
Accept or revoke citizenship
Authorize treaties, alliances, war

Article 14 — Attorney General
Role:Head of the Judicial Branch and chief legal officer.
Duties:Interpret the Constitution,Advise Congress,Ensure constitutional compliance.

Appointment:
Nominated by President, confirmed by Congress majority, 30‑day term.

Article 15 — Inactivity and Incompetence Standards

15.1 Definitions:
Inactivity: Failure to perform duties for 7+ days without notice or refusal to implement binding decisions.
Incompetence: Repeated failure harming national interests.

15.2 Reporting:
Complaints may be submitted to Congress with Evidence.

15.3 Consequences:
Congress may:
Issue warnings,
Suspend officials,
Remove officials by majority vote.

15.4 Cross‑Platform Enforcement:
Discord decisions must be implemented in‑game within 48 hours.

Article 16 — Supremacy of Constitutional Decisions

Decisions made under this Constitution on Discord are binding and must be enacted in‑game. 
Failure to implement constitutes non‑compliance.
