Preamble

We, 
The Citizens of The Republic Of India ,
in order to establish justice,
ensure peace,
promote prosperity,
and secure the freedom and
rights of all our citizens within WarEra,
hereby adopt this Constitution.


Article 1 — Sovereignty and Purpose.

Sovereignty:
The Republic Of India is a Sovereign state,
with authority derived from its citizens,
within the War Era world.

Purpose:
To protect the welfare of its citizens,
promote strategic strength, and
govern the state equitably.


Article 2 — Citizenship, Rights And Duties.

Citizenship:
Every active player who pledges allegiance and is a citizen to The Republic Of India is a citizen with rights and responsibilities.

Rights:
Citizens have the right to:
1.Participate in fair elections in game and in the Discord server of Rebuplic Of India.
2.Freedom of speech within in-game laws and discord laws with reasonable restrictions.
3.Engage in economic activity such as trade, make companies and work for Companies of thier choice.

Duties:
Citizens shall:
1.Abide by The Laws of this Nation.
2.Support the nation’s defense if called upon.
3.Respect the constitution and governance structures of the Republic of India, Whether in game or in Discord Server.


Article 3 — Government Structure

Executive Branch
Which Shall include:
President,
Vice President,
Advisor To Government Of India.

Ministery:
Ministery Of Defense,
Ministery Of Finance,
Ministery Of External Affairs,
Ministery Of Home Affairs and
Other Official Positiions.

Legislative Branch Shall Include:
Members Of The Parliament elected by citizens.
Responsible for passing laws, 
and other things as may be prescribed by the Legislative body.

Judicial Branch:
A council or elected members to resolve disputes and suggest consequences for not abiding by the Constitution Of India and laws.

Article 4 - Other official Positions.

These Positions are as follows:
RAW Director, are subordinates to the External Affairs Minister.
Information And BroadCasting Director, are subordinates to the External Affairs Minister.
Ambassadors, are subordinates to the External Affairs Minister.
Human Resource Director, are subordinates to the Home Minister.
Intelligence Directoer, are subordinates to the Home Minister.
Public Director, are subordinates to the Home Minister.


Article 5 — Elections, Appointment of minsters.

Election Process:
Elections held at designated intervals.
Candidate registration and fair voting processes is defined as per in game rules.
For the seats of President,Vice President,Defense Minister,Economy Minister,Foreign Affairs Minster.
Elections or Roles outside of in game roles shall be done in the manner prescribed by the Legislature.

Term Limits:
Officials and other official positions serve a fixed term of 30 days.
Are subject to the judiciary scrutinity if working against the intrests of India.


Article 6 — Laws and Policy

Lawmaking Procedures:
Bills and Laws may be proposed by Parliament members or Ministers.
Passage requires majority vote of the parliament members and minsiters.

Article 7- Power, Duties and chain of command of Ministers and other official positions.

President-
Powers: Has the Final say in all matters relating to India, whether internal or external, also has the power to dictate the policies of India. 	 
Duties: Answerable to the Judiciary if acting against the interest of India.  
Chain Of command: No one is above the President

Duties: Work for the safegaurd and prosperity of the India. Answerable to the Judiciary if acting against the interest of India.

Article 8 — Military and Defense

Defense Structure:
The military is Under the control of the Minister of Defense.
Which Shall Include:
1st Para SF,
10th Para SF,
Marine Commando Force,
Garud Cammando Force.

War:
Declaration of war must be approved by the legislative body.
Authority to declare war lies with the President but with approval from the in game ministers , congress members and Discord minsiters. 


Article 9 — Amendments

Amendment Process:
Amendments may be proposed and ratified with a defined super-majority by The legislative body.


Article 10 — Treaty and Alliance

External Relations:
The nation may enter or withdraw from treaties and alliances subject to legislative approval.


Article 11 — Enforcement

Compliance:
All citizens and government officials must comply with this Constitution.
Otherwise, Citizens,Minister And Directors are subject to due process of law made after the adoption of this Constitution.
Violations are addressed through judicial processes. 
Established after the adoption of this constitution.
